FAE Bot Design Specification
Main Character Features
Base Structure:
A friendly, sleek robot with clean, rounded edges for approachability.
Large, expressive digital eyes displaying warmth and intelligence.
Subtle astronaut-inspired helmet or headpiece design, with soft highlights suggesting glass or a visor.
Space Details:
Equipped with small, functional rocket boosters or anti-gravity devices on its back or sides.
Delicate constellation patterns etched subtly into its metallic body.
Thin energy trails in soft blues and cyans, creating a sense of dynamic movement.
Holographic Additions:
A small floating UI panel or hologram showcasing navigation paths, possibly including the character "飛" integrated cleverly as a motif.
Color Scheme
Primary Colors:
Soft white and metallic silver for the main body.
Accents:
Gentle blues and cyans for energy/tech elements like boosters or holograms.
Highlights:
Warm yellow/orange for friendly, inviting eye lights.
Background:
A deep space gradient (black to navy) with subtle stars for additional depth.
Style
Art Direction:
A mix of cute mascot and high-tech assistant, balancing personality with professional capabilities.
Semi-realistic textures with a cartoon-inspired edge to enhance charm.
Works effectively as both a full-figure design and a compact profile icon.
Pose and Personality
Head Pose:
Slightly tilted to convey curiosity and engagement.
Gestures:
Can be depicted holding or projecting a small navigation hologram in one hand or near its head.
Expression:
Friendly, with options for slight emotional variation (e.g., curious, focused).
Additional Features
A small communication antenna or device placed subtly on its head or shoulders.
Proportional and consistent with the established guidelines for space-themed assets:
Head-to-body ratio approximately 1:5.
Height around 300–350px for full design.
